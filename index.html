How To Play

  1. Pick a calendar year first. That decides which circuits you need to fill.
  2. Press Roll Year. The dice will land on one F1 season from 1990 to 2025.
  3. For the current circuit, choose one driver from the rolled season who you think has the best chance to win there.
  4. Driver strength changes by season. A driver can be amazing one year and weaker in another, so judge the rolled year carefully.
  5. Repeat the roll and pick process until every circuit in the calendar has a driver.
  6. Only after the full calendar is filled, press Simulate to see your record, download your card, or share it.
